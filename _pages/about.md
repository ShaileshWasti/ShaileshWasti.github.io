I am a PhD candidate in Energy Systems Engineering in the [Department of Energy and Mineral Engineering](https://www.eme.psu.edu/) at [Pennsylvania State University](https://www.psu.edu/), pursuing a minor in [Operations Research](https://www.or.psu.edu/) under the supervision of [Prof. Mort Webster](https://www.eme.psu.edu/directory/mort-d-webster). My research addresses electricity market design and energy storage operation under uncertainty, conducted in ongoing collaboration with PJM Interconnection and ISO New England. In Summer 2024, I completed a research internship in PJM's Advanced Analytics group, supervised by [Anthony Giacomoni](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anthonygiacomoni). I hold an M.S. in Electrical Engineering (Power & Energy) from the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, where I was advised by [Prof. Vahid Disfani](https://www.utc.edu/directory/czd631-college-of-engineering-and-computer-science-vahid-rasouli-disfani/czd631) and developed a distributed ADMM algorithm for energy management in smart grids.
