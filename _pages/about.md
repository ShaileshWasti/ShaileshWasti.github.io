## Shailesh Wasti
I am a PhD candidate in the [Department of Energy and Mineral Engineering](https://www.eme.psu.edu/) at [Pennsylvania State University](https://www.psu.edu/), pursuing a minor in [Operations Research](https://www.or.psu.edu/). My research focuses on electricity market design with storage under uncertainty, under the supervision of [Prof. Mort Webster](https://www.eme.psu.edu/directory/mort-d-webster). Prior to my doctoral studies, I earned an M.S. in Electrical Engineering (Power & Energy) from the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, where I was advised by [Prof. Vahid Disfani](https://www.utc.edu/directory/czd631-college-of-engineering-and-computer-science-vahid-rasouli-disfani/czd631) and worked on a [distributed ADMM algorithm for energy management in smart grids](https://scholar.utc.edu/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?article=1845&context=theses).  I also did summer internship at PJM Interconnection's Advanced Analytics group, supervised by [Anthony Giacomoni](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anthonygiacomoni).
