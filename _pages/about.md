I am a PhD candidate in the [Department of Energy and Mineral Engineering](https://www.eme.psu.edu/) at the [Pennsylvania State University](https://www.psu.edu/), with a focus in Energy Systems Engineering, working under the supervision of [Prof. Mort Webster](https://www.eme.psu.edu/directory/mort-d-webster), with a minor in [Operations Research](https://www.or.psu.edu/). My research focuses on electricity market design and energy storage operation under uncertainty. This work is conducted in ongoing collaboration with **PJM Interconnection** and **ISO New England**. In Summer 2024, I completed a research internship with PJM's Advanced Analytics group under the guidance of [Anthony Giacomoni](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anthonygiacomoni). I hold an M.S. in Electrical Engineering (Power & Energy) from the [University of Tennessee at Chattanooga](https://www.utc.edu/), where I was advised by [Prof. Vahid Disfani](https://www.utc.edu/directory/czd631-college-of-engineering-and-computer-science-vahid-rasouli-disfani/czd631).
